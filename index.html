About Us

SteppeLink Exports is a trusted transporter of premium Mongolian products — hides, furs, herbal goods, and dried dairy. We serve clients globally, bringing the best of Mongolia’s natural resources to the international market. We take pride in our commitment to **quality**, **sustainability**, and **cultural heritage**.

Hides & Leather

We provide top-quality Mongolian hides and leather, praised for their strength and unique texture. Ideal for fashion and industrial use.

Furs

Offering responsibly sourced furs from the Mongolian wilderness, perfect for high-end garments and fashion items.

Herbal Goods

Traditional Mongolian herbs and teas known for their healing properties and rich flavor, now available internationally.

Dried Dairy Products

Preserved traditional dairy products like **airag powder** and **aaruul**, renowned for their rich taste and nutritional value.
